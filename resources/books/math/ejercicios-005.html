Ecuaciones irracionales

Son aquellas donde la incógnita aparece bajo signos radicales.

Ejercicios:

  1. \[\sqrt{3x-8} -2 = 0\]
  2. \[\sqrt{2}~-\sqrt{4x+3} = 0\]
  3. \[4\sqrt{2}~-\sqrt{6x+35}= 0\]
  4. \[\sqrt{8x+6}-3\sqrt{2x-1} = 0\]
  5. \[\sqrt{4x+34}~-4\sqrt{2x+3}=0\]
  6. \[\sqrt[3]{3x^3+32}~-2=0\]
  7. \[3x-1= 3\sqrt{3x-3}\]
  8. \[\sqrt{4x-11}~+5=2x\]
  9. \[\sqrt{8x+16}~-\sqrt{4x+1}= \sqrt{4x+7}\]
  10. \[\sqrt{2x+9}~-\sqrt{8x+6}=\sqrt{2x+3}\]
Soluciones:
  1. 4
  2. -1/4
  3. -1/2
  4. 3/2
  5. -1/2
  6. -2
  7. 4/3 o 7/3
  8. 3
  9. 1/4
  10. -1/2
